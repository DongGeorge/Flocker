Use the 'MainScript' object to control the different forces
- Note, 'Has Repulsion' is used in place of 'collision avoidance' (they are the same)

To scatter the creatures, make sure 'Has Repulsion' is active and press the space key while in the game window

A max cap of 100 flying and crawling creatures can be made at a time
- Increasing the 'Boid Count' variable past this amount will not result in additional creatures
- Note, the flocking patterns of the two, flyers and crawlers, are independent
